Strategies

Opening Explorer

    Opening principles

    Control the center, develop your knights and bishops before your queen, and castle early to keep your king safe. These principles give you a strong foundation no matter what opening you play.

    Middlegame tactics

    Look for forks, pins, and skewers to win material. Always check what your opponent's last move threatens before deciding on your own plan.

    Endgame fundamentals

    Activate your king, push passed pawns, and remember that rooks belong behind passed pawns whether they are yours or your opponent's.
